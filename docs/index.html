Mask distracting speech

DubFocus is built around acoustic masking, helping make speech and abrupt sounds harder to track so your attention can settle back onto the task in front of you.

Generate sound in real time

Instead of replaying a static loop, the app reacts to your environment and produces a living soundscape that feels stable without becoming boring.

Stay in flow longer

Low-distraction textures, ambience, and binaural layers create a more predictable listening space for deep work, study, and long writing or coding sessions.

What DubFocus does

DubFocus helps turn chaotic spaces into steady auditory environments. Under the hood it blends microphone input, noise textures, ambience, oscillators, filters, modulators, and effects into a modular audio graph that stays interesting enough to support flow without competing for attention.

Built for

  • Reading and writing in shared spaces
  • Coding and technical work that demands verbal working memory
  • Study sessions in homes, offices, and cafes
  • Anyone who wants a more predictable sound environment
