Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

DubFocus is designed to work entirely on your device. This policy explains what the app accesses, why, and what it never does with that access.

Last updated: May 23, 2026

Microphone access

DubFocus requests microphone permission to generate and shape real-time acoustic masking soundscapes. The app reads the microphone signal to react to your environment and produce a dynamic audio output inside the app.

Microphone audio is processed entirely on-device in real time. It is never recorded, stored, transmitted, or shared with anyone — including the developer.

Data we do not collect

DubFocus does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Microphone audio or any audio recordings
  • Personal information (name, email address, phone number, location)
  • Usage analytics, session data, or behavioral telemetry
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Health or fitness data
  • Financial or payment information

In-app purchases

DubFocus offers an optional Premium upgrade processed entirely through Apple's App Store. All billing and payment handling is managed by Apple. DubFocus does not receive, store, or have access to any payment or financial information.

To restore a previous purchase, use the Restore Purchase option inside the app. For billing questions, contact Apple Support.

Third-party services

DubFocus does not integrate third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or social login providers. No data is shared with or sold to third parties.

Children's privacy

DubFocus does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no personal data is collected at all, the app is compatible with COPPA requirements.

Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy or how DubFocus handles data, contact the developer directly.
